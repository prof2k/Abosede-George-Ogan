Abosede George-Ogan is a tri-sector leader, having over 15 years of experience across non-profit, private, and public sectors as a development professional. From starting her career in development at a number of NGOs, to coordinating Corporate Social Responsibility for KeyStone Bank and First Bank and leading Corporate Citizenship for Samsung Electronics West Africa, Abosede has been leaving a lasting impact everywhere she goes. She currently serves as the Director, Strategy, Partnerships and Stakeholder Management at the Lagos State Employment Trust Fund.

She is also the founder of Women in Politics – a platform that engages, encourages, equips and empowers women in Nigeria to get involved and participate in politics. She’s the author of Building a Conscious Career, where she shared useful tips on how to build a fulfilling and financially rewarding career, while also positively affecting the lives of others.

Abosede has a degree in Political Science/Public Administration from Igbinedion University and an MSc in Communication for Innovation and Development from the University of Reading. She speaks on the following subject areas: Access to Finance, Youth Employment, Women's Political Participation, Sustainability and Multi-sectoral partnerships.
