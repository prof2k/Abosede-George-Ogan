Hi. I'm Abosede George-Ogan

Welcome to my website. I am a Tri-sector Leader with many interests which narrow down to a common mission - Impact. This mission is to coach, mentor and motivate people to become bigger and better versions of themselves through building for themselves a conscious career... I am also an advocate for an increased participation of women in politics in which my platform, WomenInPoliticsNg equips and mentors women to do more and take on the mantle of leadership. If this resonates with you, hop in and join me on this ride. You can find my previous and current projects and engagements. You can also find out how to get in touch for coaching, speaking and consultancy engagements. You will also find regular articles on what I am up to, things I find interesting and projects I like. I welcome you.
